How To Invest

The minimum amount for 1st time investment is Rs.10000 and in multiples of Rs.1000
Minimum amount for additional investment is Rs.5000 and in multiples of Rs.1000

To start investing, Click on Invest Link against the invester in whose name you want to invest.
Select the Payout Option.
Choose the plan - locl-in or Without Lok-in.
Choose the Minimum Holding period for which you want to make the investment.
Enter the amount, Click Invest.
On clicking Invest, you will be redirected to payment gateway page.
You can then transfer funds using Net Banking / UPI .
Once the transaction confirmation is successfull, the amount will start refecting in your portfolio within 24 Businees hours.
