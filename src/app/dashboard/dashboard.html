About Liquiloans

Liquiloans is an Indian fintech company that operates a peer-to-peer (P2P) lending platform, connecting individual borrowers with investors through a fully digital process. Founded in 2018 and headquartered in Mumbai, the company is registered as an NBFC-P2P with the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). Liquiloans focuses on providing unsecured personal loans to salaried and self-employed individuals, using technology and data-driven credit assessment models to evaluate borrower profiles and manage risk. For investors, it offers opportunities to earn returns by funding diversified loan portfolios, typically with features like auto-invest and risk-based allocation. The platform emphasizes transparency, quick loan disbursal, paperless onboarding, and customer-friendly repayment options, positioning itself as a modern alternative to traditional banking channels in India.

Why Liquilans


Liquiloans exists to bridge the gap between borrowers and investors in a faster, more efficient way than traditional banks. Here’s why platforms like Liquiloans were created:

1. Limited access to credit: Many salaried or self-employed individuals find it difficult to get quick loans from traditional banks due to strict requirements.

2. Slow banking process: Traditional loan approvals can take days or weeks. Fintech platforms use technology to approve and disburse loans much faster.

3. Better returns for investors: Instead of keeping money in low-interest savings accounts, investors can earn higher returns by lending directly to borrowers.

4. Digital convenience: Everything is online — from application to approval — making the process paperless and user-friendly.
