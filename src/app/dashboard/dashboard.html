About Liquiloans

Liquiloans is a technology-driven Indian fintech platform transforming the way individuals invest and borrow through Peer-to-Peer lending. Our platform connects creditworthy borrowers with investors seeking better, inflation-adjusted returns — all through a secure, fully digital, and transparent ecosystem.

By leveraging advanced credit assessment models, data analytics, and risk-based borrower profiling, Liquiloans ensures investments are intelligently diversified to reduce risk and enhance stability.

  • Transparent investment opportunities
  • Automated risk assessment & credit grading
  • Paperless onboarding & KYC
  • Diversified lending portfolios
  • Attractive returns vs traditional debt instruments

Our mission is to democratize credit access while empowering investors with smarter, data-backed investment solutions.
